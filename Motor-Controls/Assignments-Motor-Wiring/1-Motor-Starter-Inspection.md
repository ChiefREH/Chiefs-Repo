# MOTOR STARTER INSPECTION

Complete the following tasks:
- Disassemble the motor starter and identify the coil, internal & auxiliary contacts and thermal overloads 
- Meter and record the ohm value of the main coil 
- Inspect the contacts and contact springs for any damage or wear 
- Inspect solder pots for any damage and wear 
- Inspect the condition of the auxiliary contacts 
- Reassemble the motor starter 
- While manually operating the carriage, check for continuity between the following: 
    - Input terminals (L1, L2, L3) and output terminals (T1, T2, T3) 
    - N.C. auxiliary contacts 
    - N.O. auxiliary contacts
