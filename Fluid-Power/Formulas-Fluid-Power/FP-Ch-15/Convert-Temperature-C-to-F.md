# Converting Temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit

We are given a temperature:

- **Temperature:** 327 °C  
- **Goal:** Convert to **Fahrenheit (°F)**

---

## Step 1: Understand the relationship

The formula to convert **Celsius to Fahrenheit** is:

    °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Where:  
- °C is temperature in Celsius  
- °F is temperature in Fahrenheit

---

## Step 2: Identify the values

- °C = 327

---

## Step 3: Apply the formula

    °F = (327 × 9/5) + 32

---

## Step 4: Multiply by 9/5 (or 1.8)

    327 × 9/5 = 327 × 1.8 = 588.6

---

## Step 5: Add 32

    588.6 + 32 = 620.6

---

## Step 6: Include units

    Temperature = 620.6 °F

---

## Answer

Temperature = 620.6 °F
