# Create a basic Extend-Center-Retract signal loop:

- Send a signal of "+1" for 5 seconds
- Send a signal of "0" for 5 seconds
- Send a signal of "-1" for 5 seconds
- Send a signal of "0" for 5 seconds
- Repeat continuously
