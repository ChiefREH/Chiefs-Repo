# BASIC PUSHBUTTON WIRING

For this example, power moves from left to right

- The internal contacts are arranged in pairs horizontally (parallel)

![Pushbutton Contacts](Assignment-Motors-Basic-Images/Pushbutton-Pairs.png)

Wire (arrow) comes in from the left and goes out the right...

![NC good](Assignment-Motors-Basic-Images/Good-Wiring-1.png)

![NO good](Assignment-Motors-Basic-Images/Good-Wiring-2.png)

You can wire in/out on the NC and NO at the same time!

![Both good](Assignment-Motors-Basic-Images/Good-Wiring-3.png)

You CANNOT wire in on one pair and out on another pair (diagonally)

![Not good](Assignment-Motors-Basic-Images/Bad-Wiring-1.png)

![Not good](Assignment-Motors-Basic-Images/Bad-Wiring-2.png)
